Why Participate

Join the next generation of "provable and controllable" AI, compete with top teams, and produce reproducible, auditable, and practical research and system outcomes.

High-Impact Topics

Focus on cutting-edge directions like automated theorem proving, scientific discovery agents, multimodal symbolic reasoning, and program synthesis & verification.

Systematic Evaluation

Multi-dimensional metrics for correctness, robustness, and traceability, emphasizing end-to-end processes and standardized artifacts.

Generous Incentives

Grand champion, track champions, and special awards, with opportunities for showcasing and collaboration.

Tracks Overview

Scientific Discovery Agents

Multimodal Symbolic Reasoning

Symbol extraction and verifiable reasoning across text, charts, and equations.
